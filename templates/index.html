How It Works

From raw data to intelligent predictions — our pipeline explained

1️⃣

Data Collection

23,427 real job postings across 5 categories with extracted skill sets form our training dataset.

2️⃣

Feature Engineering

Skills are cleaned, normalized, and converted to numerical features using TF-IDF vectorization (5,000 features).

3️⃣

Model Training

A Random Forest with 200 decision trees is trained on 80% of the data, achieving {{ "%.1f"|format(accuracy) }}% accuracy.
